Email Hosting Services FAQs

Find answers to frequently asked questions about email hosting for businesses

What is email hosting?

Email hosting is a dedicated service that works independently from website hosting. So while setting up custom email addresses, your website from the current web hosting provider will be able to operate at full scale.

What are the advantages of domain-based email accounts?

Nowadays, establishing brand trust and awareness is crucial. Numerous studies and surveys show that customers are more likely to do business with those who use professional email addresses.
While anyone can create a free email account, a custom email address branded with your own domain name will separate you from spammers and scammers. Note that most web hosting providers, including Hostinger, even give away a free domain-based email address when you buy a hosting plan.

Can I migrate my own email and contacts from third-party email hosting providers?

Of course! Our professional email plans come with a built-in migration tool to help you move email data onto our platform, whether the hosted email service is provided by your own mail server, Zoho mail, Microsoft Exchange, or Google Workspace.
For easy management, you can migrate both domain and email hosting services. If you run into trouble with the migration at any point, our dedicated Customer Success team is available 24/7!

Can I set up email accounts on Apple and Android devices?

Yes. Titan provides native email client apps for both Apple and Android devices.
